Volunteer

The Twin Cities Kids in Need Resource Center could not operate without volunteers, we need your help!

Teachers love coming to the Twin Cities Resource Center to shop for FREE supplies for less fortunate students in their classrooms, classroom projects, and incentive items.

We believe when a child has the necessary supplies the first day of school, it gives them the confidence they need to succeed in the world of tomorrow.

Volunteering not only benefits us here at the Resource Center, but many deserving kids and teachers in the Twin Cities area. It certainly is a wonderful way to give back to the community and we appreciate it VERY much!
